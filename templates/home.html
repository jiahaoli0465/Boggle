Welcome to Boggle!

Boggle is a classic word game that challenges you to find words in sequences of adjacent letters on a grid.

Made by Jiahao Li

How to Play

  • The game begins with a 5x5 grid of letters. Your task is to find words by chaining letters together.
  • Each word must be at least three letters long.
  • Letters must be adjacent to one another, and you cannot use a single letter tile more than once per word.
  • Words can be formed from letters that are horizontal, vertical, or diagonal to each other.
  • Try to find as many words as possible before the time runs out!
